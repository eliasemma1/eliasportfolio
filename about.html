About Me

Building Machines.
Solving Problems.

Mechanical Design Engineer & Robotics Innovator — Lagos, Nigeria

I'm Emmanuel Elias, a Mechanical Engineering student with a deep focus on robotics, mechatronic systems, and precision CAD design. My work sits at the intersection of classical mechanical engineering and modern embedded technology.

My journey started with a childhood fascination with how things move — gears, levers, mechanisms. That curiosity evolved into building real systems: ground robots, robotic arms, custom drones, and assistive devices. I don't just design on screen — I build, test, iterate, and improve.

I believe great engineering is about constraint-driven creativity: the best solutions are elegant not despite their limits, but because of them. Every project teaches me something new about design, manufacturing, and what it means to solve real problems.
